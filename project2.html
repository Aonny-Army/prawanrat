Project 02

Accident Situation Notification Application

View Mobile Prototype

Project Overview

Omni Alarm is a senior project in which the creator was responsible for both UX/UI design and actual application development. The app aims to provide real-time accident and emergency notifications to nearby users, helping them stay informed and avoid danger efficiently.

The system enables users to receive alerts, get emergency instructions, and access assistance details via a mobile interface. Built using Dart (Flutter) and PHP (with a local database), the application was fully implemented and tested using black-box testing to ensure functional reliability.

Problem Statement

Currently, accident and emergency notifications often lack real-time delivery and accessibility, especially in areas without advanced alert systems.

As a result, people may not be aware of or prepared for sudden incidents, increasing the risk of harm to life and property. Moreover, there is no all-in-one mobile application that provides timely alerts, safety guidance, and emergency support information in an integrated and user-friendly format.

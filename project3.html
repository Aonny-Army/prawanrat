Project 03

Pet Health Log

View Mobile Prototype

Project Overview

Pet Health Log is a conceptual app designed to resemble a personal note-taking application, but specifically tailored for pet owners to log important information about their beloved pets. It includes basic features such as health notes, vet appointments, and pet profiles.

A preliminary idea for a pet product shopping section was also explored to suggest future expansion. This project focuses on early stage UI/UX design, covering the core user flow and key interactions.

Problem Statement

There is currently a lack of simple and dedicated tools for pet owners to log and organize pet-related information in the same convenient way people use general note-taking apps.

As a result, many owners rely on scattered or manual methods, which makes tracking appointments or health updates inefficient.

The Pet Health Log concept was designed to address this gap by providing a structured, user-friendly way to record and access pet information.
